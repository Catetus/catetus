Catetus — CodecGS-Lite demo

Loads a .glb with the SF_codecgs_lite extension, extracts the H.264 video bitstream from each bufferView, decodes via WebCodecs.VideoDecoder, and dequantizes the frames back into splat attributes. Proof that the codec route is decodable in-browser.
